Gas to Electricity

Currently, Victoria supports some electrification projects but still relies on natural gas. To reduce these manufacturing emissions, we can take a similar approach. The government can reduce the amount of money they tax companies if they change their manufacturing processes to run on 100% electricity. We can also start to force companies to shift from traditional heating methods to hydrogen heating methods, providing subsidies so help speed up the process. And also pass a law that all new industrial building must run on electricity
