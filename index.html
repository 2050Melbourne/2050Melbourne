2050 MELBOURNE

What are we about?

2050 Melbourne is an idea that by 2050 in Melbourne we will have reduced our carbon emission levels to an almost non existent level.

Our website aims to provide an overview of the cause of carbon levels, the problems they create, and a solution to reducing carbon levels but in a way that doesn't affect our lives with a major negative impact.

Conclusion

In conclusion, we face many issues and dangers as a result of carbon emissions, including natural disasters and damage to the environment and its ecosystems. These carbon emissions are present as a result of the heavy widespread industrialisation that took place during the Second Industrial Revolution, which increased reliance on fossil fuels and carbon producing chemical processes. In order to combat this, we must achieve our goal of reaching net zero by 2050. To do this, we must take action, by banning old vehicles that run on fuel, offering financial incentives for electric homes, using electricity instead of gas, and more. Overall, while the climate issues we face today may seem daunting, it is still possible for us to prevent further damage so that Australia, its people, and its ecosystems can continue thrive in the future.
